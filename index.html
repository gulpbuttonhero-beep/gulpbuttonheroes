Safety & transparency

We‚Äôre serious about safety, consent, and responsible installs. These rooms serve families in sensitive situations. Any improvements should be durable, maintainable, and appropriate for the environment.

Are you affiliated with DCYF / CPS?

Not by default. This is a community-led volunteer effort. Any collaboration with agencies or facilities would require formal permission, coordination, and safety standards.

What kinds of upgrades are you talking about?

Practical, durable improvements: wipe-clean furniture, storage, kid-safe activity panels, books, calming color, art surfaces, modular features designed for easy maintenance and replacement.

How do you handle donations (money or materials)?

Monetary donations go through the Donate button on this page. In-kind donations (materials/supplies/services) go through the In-Kind form. We aim to be transparent, avoid waste, and prioritize safe, useful items.

Sometimes‚Äîif items are clean, safe, and appropriate. Many facilities have strict hygiene and safety rules. The In-Kind form helps us screen items and coordinate what‚Äôs actually usable.
